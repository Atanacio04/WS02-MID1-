About Cid Kagenou

formerly Minoru Kagenō, is the titular antihero and main protagonist of The Eminence in Shadow series. He is the leader of the secret organization Shadow Garden under the moniker Shadow, which he founded to live out his chūnibyō fantasies, unaware that his imaginary enemies actually exist.

Power & Abilities

Cid is described as possibly 'the most powerful being in the world,' be it martial arts or magic, to the point of it being doubted whether he is even human. In fact, he is actually the strongest in several worlds, defeating a demon from another dimension without much difficulty. Despite having yet to demonstrate his full strength, all of Cid's battles so far have ended in overwhelming victories, easily defeating high-ranking Cult of Diablos and Knights of Rounds members, with the only opponents he had to get a "little serious" against being the Mist Dragon, Aurora's projection, Beatrix, Elisabeth, and the aforementioned demon.

  1. Superhuman Physical Powers

    2. Cid is an extremely powerful warrior with physical strength strong enough to shatter concrete with just the shockwaves of his attacks

  2. Superhuman Senses

    3. Through training in his childhood and experimentation with his magic both on himself and otherwise, Cid developed heightened human senses beyond what a human, whether a magical warrior or normal person, can. have, having a better range of vision, better hearing, and better control of the touch of things.

    4. Despite his defects of Chunnibyou, he has proven to have and developed a cunningness capable of advanced calculations at the level of elite soldiers.
